Welcome to CamView,

A web application that allows you to manipulate video footage.

    CamView consists of three windows:
  • Filters: Allows you to apply predefined filters and custom slider webkit filters.
  • Viewport: Camera source display.
  • Capture: Settings for photo capturing and exporting, capturing settings such as: flash, timer, timelapse, etc.

    • *(Note: webkitfilters are currently not supported for capture exports.)
